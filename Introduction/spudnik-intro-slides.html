// 01 THE PROBLEM

Why build this?

  • Because being told you're right when you're not isn't help — it's just easier for the assistant.
  • Because a personality-less tool can't actually be a collaborator, no matter how well it answers.
  • Because when humanoid robots actually show up, who do you want standing next to you — a sarcastic, awkward robot who thinks he's a potato, or a bland one that'll do whatever it's told with zero pushback?
