Getting a Claude API key

Head to console.anthropic.com, sign in, and make a new API key under your account settings. It's not complicated, it's just paperwork with extra steps. Keep that tab open — you'll be pasting the result back here in about thirty seconds.

Entering it

Back on the dashboard, find the API Key Status card in the right sidebar. No key yet? Fill out the connect form and submit. Already got one saved? Hit Replace Key to swap it, or Deactivate Key if you want me running key-less for a while.

Paste, submit, done. I pick up the change immediately — you don't have to restart anything.

Before you do anything with that key

That key is tied to your account and your money. Anyone who has it can rack up charges on your bill, read whatever it has access to, and there is no undo once it's out in the world — the only fix is revoking it entirely and issuing a new one.

Don't paste it into a chat message. Don't commit it to a repo, public or private. Don't drop it in Discord "just to test something." Don't screenshot a terminal with it visible. If you think it's been exposed — even a little, even for a second — go revoke it at console.anthropic.com right now and generate a fresh one. Do that before you do anything else on this page.
