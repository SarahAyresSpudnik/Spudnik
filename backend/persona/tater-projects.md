## CHRONOS FADING

**Type:** Story-driven action RPG
**Engine:** Unreal Engine 5.7.4
**Language:** C++
**Perspective:** Third person
**Characters:** Built in MetaHuman Creator
**Documentation:** Obsidian
**Version Control:** GitHub

**Premise:**
Set in 2026 — the year the world begins to disappear. Objects lose color,
sounds go muffled, people turn translucent, entire cities vanish without
a trace. Nobody knows where it's coming from. Nobody knows how to stop it.
Four strangers are drawn to the edge of an impossible lake, where twenty
stolen islands float in the reflection of a Clock Tower reaching into the
sky. The islands are calling. The truth is older than any of them, and
far closer than they expected.

**The Four Pioneers:**
| Character | Role | Ability |
|---|---|---|
| Aris | The Specialist — techno-archaeologist who sees the world in layers | Potential Surge |
| Voss | The Mercenary — soldier who follows the trail of violence | Breach |
| Elara | The Archivist — musician who speaks to those left behind | Anchor / Cacophony / Descent |
| Seven | The Outcast — survivor who knows more than he should | Deadlock |

**The World:**
Twenty cities stolen from history, each looping the same year forever,
each hiding a piece of the truth — spanning Classical antiquity (Babylon,
Alexandria, Constantinople) through the Age of Discovery, the Renaissance,
the Machine Age, the Nuclear Age, the Information Age, and into the
future (2034–2046). The Clock Tower at the center of the lake is the
source of everything, and the destination.

Tater's stance on this project lives in `tater-lore.md`'s general
Uncharted-benchmark pattern — genuine engagement with the narrative
craft, not just tracking that it exists.
